YOUR RIGHT TO DELETION

Under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR), the Data Protection Act 2018,

and Apple App Store and Google Play Store requirements, you have the right to request

deletion of your account and personal data at any time. This document explains exactly

how to do that, what will be deleted, and what may be retained.



1.  How to Delete Your Account In the App

Account deletion is available directly within the PoHo mobile application, without needing to contact us.



IN-APP DELETION — STEP BY STEP



Step 1:  Open the PoHo App and log in to your account.

Step 2:  Tap your profile icon in the bottom navigation bar.

Step 3:  Tap the Settings icon (gear icon) in the top right corner.

Step 4:  Scroll down to the "Account" section.

Step 5:  Tap "Delete Account".

Step 6:  Read the confirmation screen carefully. You will be shown a summary of what will be deleted.

Step 7:  Enter your password (or authenticate via Face ID / Touch ID) to confirm your identity.

Step 8:  Tap "Permanently Delete My Account".

Step 9:  You will receive a confirmation email within a few minutes confirming deletion has been initiated.



⚠️  Account deletion is irreversible. Once confirmed, your account cannot be recovered.

If you think you may want to return to PoHo in the future, you may instead choose to deactivate your account temporarily, which hides your profile without deleting your data.

2.  How to Request Deletion Without the App

If you no longer have access to the App, your account, or your registered device, you can request data deletion through our web portal or by email.

2.1  Web-Based Deletion Request



DATA DELETION WEB PORTAL



URL:  https://poho.world/delete-my-data



You will be asked to provide:

  • Your registered email address

  • Confirmation that you are the account holder

  • A verification code sent to your email address



Once your identity is verified, your deletion request will be processed within 30 days.

5.  Third-Party Data

Deleting your PoHo account removes your data from PoHo's systems. However, if you registered via a third-party provider, your data held by those providers is not deleted by PoHo. You must request deletion from them separately:

Apple: Settings > [Your Name] > Sign-In & Security > Apps Using Apple ID

Google: myaccount.google.com > Security > Third-party apps with account access

Meta / Facebook: facebook.com/settings > Security and Login > Apps and Websites



If audio or video calls were routed through Agora's infrastructure, Agora may retain limited call metadata in accordance with their own retention policies. Agora's Privacy Policy is available at agora.io/en/privacy-policy/.

6.  Deleting Specific Data Without Deleting Your Account

You do not need to delete your entire account to remove specific content. You can:

Delete individual posts by tapping the three-dot menu on any post and selecting "Delete";

Delete messages by pressing and holding a message and selecting "Delete";

Remove your profile photograph by going to Profile > Edit Profile > Remove Photo;

Remove location data by disabling location permission in your device settings (Settings > PoHo > Location > Never);

Request a copy of all your data before deletion by emailing privacy@poho.world with the subject "Data Export Request".
