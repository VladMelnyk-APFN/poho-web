3.  Description of Services

PoHo is built around action: helping users discover events and activities, find companions to attend them with, and participate in real-world experiences. The Services are structured around three core areas:

3.1  Events and Activities

Users may discover, create, join, and organise social activities including group dinners, sports sessions, casual meetups, cultural outings, and other shared experiences. Events are typically designed as small, manageable group experiences to reduce social barriers to participation.

3.2  Social and Dating

PoHo supports social connection and dating through an activity-first model. Rather than focusing on profile browsing or extended messaging, the platform encourages connection through shared experiences. Users may be matched with others attending the same event or express interest in attending an event with a specific user.

3.3  Travel and Exploration

The platform supports users who wish to explore new cities or locations, whether as visitors or locals. Users may find travel companions, connect with others in the same location, and discover activities relevant to their interests.

3.4  Communication Features

The Services include real-time text messaging via WebSocket technology and audio/video calling via Agora Real-Time Communication (RTC) infrastructure, integrated with native device operating system call interfaces (Apple CallKit on iOS and CallKeep on Android).
